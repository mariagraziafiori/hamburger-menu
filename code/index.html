Welcome to the University of Zurich

University

With its 28,000 enrolled students, the University of Zurich (UZH) is Switzerland's largest university.

Find here all the most important information about the University of Zurich and its educating programs.

Studies

Browse or search the course catalogue of all degree programs at the University of Zurich.

Internal and external advisory services can help you decide what course to choose.

Research

The University of Zurich is a leading player on the European research scene, and in certain areas ranks among the very best in the world.

Its Research Priority Programs define areas of focus for the future.

Outreach

Find out about libraries, public lectures and events at the University of Zurich.

The Main Library of the University of Zurich and the numerous public libraries at UZH's institutes and departments hold over two million volumes.
